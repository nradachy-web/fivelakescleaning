Why Post-Construction Cleaning is Different

Construction Debris Requires a Specialized Approach

Standard cleaning services are not equipped for what a construction or renovation project leaves behind. Fine drywall dust migrates into every cabinet, drawer, and duct. Adhesive residue bonds to glass and tile. Grout haze clouds brand-new floors. Paint overspray settles on fixtures that were never meant to be touched. If it is not addressed correctly and in the right order, these residues can scratch surfaces, clog HVAC systems, and turn a pristine build into a headache before the first occupant moves in.

Five Lakes Cleaning has developed a systematic, multi-phase approach designed specifically for post-construction environments. Our crews understand construction materials, surface compatibilities, and the order of operations that protects your finishes while achieving a spotless result. We work closely with general contractors, specialty trades, homeowners completing renovations, and property developers throughout the Pontiac area and all of Oakland County.

Whether you are handing off a new custom home build, completing a commercial tenant improvement, or finishing a whole-home renovation, we deliver the final clean that makes your project shine.

Our Process

Four-Phase Post-Construction Clean

Every post-construction project follows the same disciplined sequence to protect surfaces and ensure nothing is missed.

1

Rough Clean and Debris Removal

We start by removing all large debris, scrap material, packing materials, protective films, and construction waste from every surface and floor. This phase clears the field so detail work can begin without redistribution of large particles. We sweep, vacuum, and bag construction waste for proper disposal.

2

Dust Elimination and Surface Wipe-Down

Construction dust, and drywall dust in particular, is ultra-fine and relentless. We HEPA-vacuum every surface from ceiling to floor, including walls, ceiling fans, light fixtures, windowsills, cabinetry interiors, and inside every closet and built-in. We then wipe down all hard surfaces with microfiber cloths designed to capture rather than redistribute fine particulates.

3

Detail Cleaning and Residue Removal

This is where post-construction cleaning diverges most sharply from standard cleaning. Our team removes paint overspray, adhesive labels, sticker residue, caulk smears, grout haze, mortar drips, and construction adhesive from windows, glass, tile, fixtures, and hardware. We use surface-appropriate solvents and tools to lift residue without scratching or etching delicate finishes.

4

Final Polish and Walkthrough

With all residue removed, our team performs a complete final polish. Floors are mopped and buffed, stainless steel appliances and fixtures are polished, glass is cleaned streak-free inside and out, and every room is inspected top to bottom. We do not sign off until the space looks exactly the way a completed project should: perfect.
