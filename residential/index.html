What's Included in Every Residential Clean

No cutting corners. No skipping the hard spots. Every visit follows our comprehensive checklist so you always know exactly what you're getting.

Kitchen

  • Countertops and backsplash wiped and sanitized
  • Stovetop, burners, and range hood degreased
  • Sink scrubbed, faucets polished
  • Appliance exteriors wiped down (refrigerator, microwave, dishwasher)
  • Cabinet fronts wiped
  • Floor swept, mopped, and dried
  • Trash emptied and liner replaced

Bathrooms

  • Toilet scrubbed inside and out, including base and behind
  • Tub, shower walls, and glass doors scrubbed and descaled
  • Sink and faucet scrubbed and polished
  • Mirror and glass cleaned streak-free
  • Counters and surfaces wiped and disinfected
  • Floor mopped and dried
  • Trash emptied and liner replaced

Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • Dusting of all surfaces, furniture tops, shelves, and decor
  • Ceiling fans and light fixtures dusted
  • Baseboards and window sills wiped down
  • Beds made (linens changed on request)
  • Mirrors and glass surfaces wiped streak-free
  • Carpets vacuumed (hard floors swept and mopped)
  • Trash emptied throughout
