Our Complete Move-Out Cleaning Checklist

Every move-in / move-out clean includes this comprehensive checklist. No add-on fees, no shortcuts. This is the same standard checklist our clients hand to their landlords to pre-empt disputes before they start.

🍳 Kitchen

  • Deep clean oven interior, racks, and door glass
  • Stovetop, burner grates, and drip pans
  • Refrigerator inside, shelves, drawers, and seals
  • Microwave inside and out
  • All cabinet and drawer interiors and exteriors
  • Countertops, backsplash, and sink
  • Dishwasher interior and door seal

🛁 Bathrooms

  • Scrub and descale shower, tub, and tile grout
  • Toilet interior, exterior, base, and behind
  • Vanity, sink, and faucet fixtures
  • Mirror and glass cleaning
  • Cabinet interiors, shelving, and medicine cabinet

🛏 Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • All closet interiors, rods, and shelves
  • Ceiling fans and light fixtures
  • Baseboards, window sills, and ledges
  • Interior windows and tracks
  • Vacuum and mop all flooring

🏠 Whole Home

  • Interior doors, frames, and handles
  • Light switches and outlet covers
  • Walls spot-cleaned for marks and scuffs
  • Garage floor and laundry area
  • Final walkthrough inspection

Our Process

Simple, Stress-Free Booking

1

Get a Free Quote

Call us at (586) 872-6830 or fill out our online form. Tell us your square footage, number of bedrooms and bathrooms, and your move date. We'll give you a straightforward price with no hidden fees.

2

Schedule Your Clean

Pick a date and time that works around your move. We offer flexible morning and afternoon windows, including weekends. We recommend scheduling your move-out clean after your belongings are out so we can access every surface, but we can work with furniture present if needed.

3

We Clean. You Relax.

Our trained team arrives on time with all equipment and eco-friendly supplies included. We work systematically through our checklist, tackling the toughest areas, including oven interiors, grout lines, and behind appliances, so nothing gets skipped.

4

Final Walkthrough and Sign-Off

Before we leave, we walk through the property with you (or with your landlord's checklist in hand) to verify every item is complete. If anything doesn't meet your expectations, we re-clean it on the spot. Your satisfaction is guaranteed before we close the door.
