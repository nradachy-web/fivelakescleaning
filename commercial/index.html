What's Included in Every Commercial Clean

Our standard commercial cleaning scope covers all the high-priority areas your team and clients interact with daily. Every visit includes:

General Areas & Offices

  • Vacuuming carpets and rugs
  • Sweeping and mopping hard floors
  • Dusting desks, shelves, and surfaces
  • Wiping down chairs and furniture
  • Emptying trash and replacing liners
  • Cleaning glass partitions and interior windows
  • Sanitizing high-touch points (door handles, light switches, elevator buttons)

Restrooms

  • Disinfecting toilets, urinals, and sinks
  • Wiping mirrors and countertops
  • Restocking paper towels, toilet paper, and soap
  • Mopping and sanitizing floors
  • Removing trash and replacing liners

Break Rooms & Common Areas

  • Wiping countertops, tables, and appliance exteriors
  • Cleaning microwave interiors and exteriors
  • Sanitizing sinks and faucets
  • Sweeping and mopping floors
  • Removing trash and recycling
  • Lobby and reception area presentation cleaning

Need something added? Every plan is customizable. We'll build a scope of work that matches your facility's specific needs and schedule.
